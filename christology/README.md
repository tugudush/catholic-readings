# Christology: The Person and Work of Jesus Christ

Welcome to the **Christology** directory of the Holy Agent repository. This directory gathers the Church's authoritative teachings, theological reflections, and devotional materials concerning the **Person and work of Jesus Christ** — the eternal Son of God, the Second Person of the Blessed Trinity, who "for us men and for our salvation came down from heaven, and by the Holy Spirit was incarnate of the Virgin Mary, and became man" (Nicene-Constantinopolitan Creed).

Christology is the central mystery of the Christian faith: the **Incarnation** of the Word, the **hypostatic union** of the divine and human natures in the one Person of Jesus Christ, and the **redemptive work** of His life, Passion, death, Resurrection, and Ascension. This directory addresses both the dogmatic foundations of Christology and the devotional life that flows from contemplation of Christ in His various mysteries and aspects.
